Factory, ek three-year-old startup, ne $150 million ki funding raise ki hai, jo Khosla Ventures dwara lead kiya gaya hai. Is funding ke saath, Factory ka valuation $1.5B par pahunch gaya hai. 

 Yeh startup AI coding ke liye enterprises ko taiyaar karne par focus karta hai. Factory ka maqsad hai ki enterprises ko AI-powered coding tools pradaan kare, jisse unki productivity badh sake. 

 Factory ke founder ne kaha, 'Humare AI-powered coding tools se enterprises ko apne software development process mein sudhar lane mein madad milegi.' 

 Factory ki funding mein shamil hain Khosla Ventures, ke alawa aur bhi kai investors. Is funding se Factory ko apne products aur services ko aur bhi behtar banane mein madad milegi. 

 Aaj kal ki duniya mein AI technology bahut tezi se badh rahi hai, aur enterprises ko bhi iska fayda uthana hoga. Factory jaise startups ki Zaroorat hai, jo enterprises ko AI-powered coding tools pradaan kar sake.
