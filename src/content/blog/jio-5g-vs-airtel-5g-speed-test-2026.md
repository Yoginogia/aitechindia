| **Rural Areas** | ⚠️ Expanding | ❌ Very limited |
| **Indoor** | ✅ Good (n28 band) | ⚠️ Average |

### Jio Ka Advantage: n28 Band
Jio n28 (700 MHz) band use karta hai jo:
- **Better indoor coverage** deta hai
- **Longer range** hai (walls penetrate karta hai)
- Lekin speed thodi kam hoti hai

### Airtel Ka Advantage: C-Band (n78)
Airtel primarily n78 (3500 MHz) band use karta hai:
- **Higher speeds** milti hain
- **Lower latency**
- Lekin indoor coverage weak hai
