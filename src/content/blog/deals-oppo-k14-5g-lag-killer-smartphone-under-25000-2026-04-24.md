## 🎮 "Lag Killer" — Kya Sach Mein Lag Nahi Hota?

OPPO ne is phone ko **"Lag Killer"** tagline ke saath launch kiya hai. Dekhte hain kya features is claim ko justify karte hain:

### 1. Snapdragon 6 Gen 3 Processor
Qualcomm ka ye chipset mid-range ka **sabse powerful processor** hai 2026 mein:
* **4nm fabrication** — energy efficient aur fast
* **Adreno 710 GPU** — BGMI, Free Fire, Genshin Impact smooth chalenge
* **AI Engine** — smart app optimization, battery management
* **AnTuTu Score:** ~650,000 — is price mein impressive

### 2. 8GB + 8GB Virtual RAM
* Physical **8GB LPDDR4X** RAM hai
* Software ke through **8GB Virtual RAM** add hoti hai storage se
* Total **16GB effective RAM** — 15-20 apps background mein open rakh sakte ho
* App switching mein koi lag nahi aayega
* Gaming ke dauran bhi notification check kar sakte ho bina game close kiye

### 3. 120Hz AMOLED Display
* **120Hz refresh rate** — scrolling butter-smooth hogi
* **AMOLED panel** — deep blacks, vibrant colors, always-on display
* **1080x2400 resolution** — text sharp dikhega
* **1000 nits peak brightness** — dhoop mein bhi clearly dikhega
* **TUV Rheinland certified** — aankhen jaldi nahi thakenge

### 4. Liquid Cooling System
* **VC (Vapor Chamber) Cooling** — gaming ke dauran processor ka temperature control mein rehta hai
* **Graphite sheet** additional heat dissipation ke liye
* 30 minute BGMI ke baad bhi phone comfortably hold kar sakte ho
* Thermal throttling minimal hai — consistent performance milti hai
