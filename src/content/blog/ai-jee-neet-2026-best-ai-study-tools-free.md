JEE Mains, JEE Advanced, aur NEET — India ke sabse tough exams! Lekin 2026 mein **AI tools** ne preparation ka game completely badal diya hai. Ab **₹0 mein** wo resources milte hain jo pehle ₹1-2 lakh ki coaching mein milte the!

## 🎯 JEE/NEET Preparation Ke Liye Best AI Tools

### 1. 🧮 ChatGPT — Physics & Maths Problem Solver

**JEE/NEET Use Cases:**

**Physics:**
* **"JEE Advanced level ka Electrostatics ka numerical solve karo step by step"**
* **"HC Verma Chapter 5 ke concepts simple language mein explain karo"**
* **"Rotational Motion ke top 10 tricky questions banao"**

**Chemistry:**
* **"Organic Chemistry ke Named Reactions ki list do with mechanisms"**
* **"Inorganic Chemistry ke exceptions jo JEE mein aate hain — list do"**
* **"Chemical Bonding ke 15 MCQs do — JEE Mains pattern mein"**

**Biology (NEET):**
* **"Human Physiology ke important diagrams list karo — NEET perspective se"**
* **"Genetics ke Mendel's laws explain karo with examples aur exceptions"**

### 💡 Topper Strategy:
Daily raat ko ChatGPT se **"Aaj maine jo padha usse 10 questions banao"** — Self-testing se retention 3x badhti hai!

---

### 2. 📸 Google Gemini — Diagram & Visual Learning

**JEE/NEET Special Features:**

* **Textbook photo upload** karke instant explanation lo
* **Complex diagrams** samjho — Heart diagram, Circuit diagrams, Crystal structures
* **NCERT page photo** do → AI important points highlight karega
* **Comparison tables** banwao — "ATP vs ADP difference table banao"

### 💡 Topper Strategy:
Har chapter padhne ke baad Gemini se bolo: **"Is chapter ka one-page summary banao jo revision mein kaam aaye"** — Exam se pehle sirf ye summaries padho!

---

### 3. 🔬 Wolfram Alpha — Mathematical & Scientific Computing

**Kyun Zaruri hai JEE ke liye:**

* **Complex integrals** solve karta hai step-by-step
* **Graph plot** karta hai functions ke
* **Chemical equations** balance karta hai automatically
* **Physics formulas** verify kar sakte ho

**Example:** Type karo: integrate x^2 * sin(x) dx — Poora solution steps ke saath!

**Website:** [wolframalpha.com](https://wolframalpha.com) (Free)

---

### 4. 📱 Perplexity AI — Latest Exam Updates & Research

**JEE/NEET Ke Liye Kyun Best:**

* **Real-time information** — "JEE Mains 2026 exam date kya hai?" — Sahi answer with source
* **Cutoff predictions** — "NIT Trichy CSE cutoff 2026 expected" — Data-backed answer
* **College comparisons** — "IIT Bombay vs IIT Delhi CSE compare karo" — Detailed analysis
* **Scholarship info** — Latest scholarships automatically find karta hai

---

### 5. 🎥 YouTube AI Summaries (Chrome Extension)

**Kaise Use Karo:**

1. **Glasp** ya **NoteGPT** Chrome extension install karo
2. Koi bhi JEE/NEET lecture video kholo YouTube pe
3. Extension **automatically transcript summarize** karega
4. **Key points** aur **timestamps** milenge

### 💡 Topper Strategy:
2-hour lecture ka **10-minute summary** padho for quick revision!
