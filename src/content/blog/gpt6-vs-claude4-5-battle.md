2026 shuru hote hi AI ki duniya mein ek nayi jung chid gayi hai. OpenAI ne apna **GPT-6** announce kiya hai, toh wahi Anthropic ne **Claude 4.5** ke sath market mein entry li hai. 

## GPT-6: Next-Gen Reasoning
GPT-6 sirf ek chatbot nahi hai. Isme "Recursive Self-Correction" feature hai jiska matlab hai ki ye code likhte waqt apni galtiyan khud pehchaan kar unhe fix kar sakta hai. Iska context window ab 5 Million tokens tak pahunch gaya hai.

## Claude 4.5: Emotional Intelligence & Speed
Claude 4.5 ka focus human-like interaction par zyada hai. Iska 'Natural Voice Engine' itna real hai ki aap differentiate nahi kar payenge ki ye AI hai ya asli insaan. 

**Hamari Ray:** Agar aapko deep research aur coding karni hai toh GPT-6 best hai, par creative writing aur client handling ke liye Claude 4.5 king hai.
