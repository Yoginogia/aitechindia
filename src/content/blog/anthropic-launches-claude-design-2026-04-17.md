Anthropic ne apna naya product Claude Design launch kiya hai, jo logon ko unke ideas ko asaani se share karne mein madad karta hai. Yeh product khaskar founders aur product managers ke liye banaya gaya hai jo design background nahi rakhte. Claude Design ka istemal karke, yeh log apne ideas ko jaldi se visuals mein badal sakte hain aur apne team ke saath asaani se share kar sakte hain.

 Claude Design ki khubsurti yeh hai ki yeh aapko design background ki zarurat nahi hai. Yeh product aapko ek simple aur intuitive interface provide karta hai, jiske through aap apne ideas ko jaldi se visuals mein badal sakte hain. Iske alawa, Claude Design mein kai pre-made templates aur design elements bhi hain, jo aapko apne visuals ko aur bhi attractive banane mein madad karte hain.

 Anthropic ka kehna hai ki Claude Design ka maqsad logon ko unke ideas ko asaani se share karne mein madad karna hai. Company ka kehna hai ki yeh product founders aur product managers ke liye khaskar upyogi hai, jo design background nahi rakhte. Lekin yeh product kisi bhi vyakti ke liye upyogi hai jo apne ideas ko jaldi se visuals mein badalna chahta hai.

 Claude Design ab tak ke liye sirf limited access mein hai, lekin company ne bataya hai ki yeh jaldi hi public ke liye available hoga. Agar aap Claude Design ko ajmane mein ruchi rakhte hain, to aap Anthropic ki website par jakar apna interest register kar sakte hain.
