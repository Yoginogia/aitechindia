AI Anxiety Gap: OpenAI ki Shopping Spree aur Tokenmaxxing

Aaj kal, AI ke baare mein sabse zyada charcha ho rahi hai. Lekin AI insiders aur baki logon ke beech ka gap badhta ja raha hai, aur kharch, shak aur naya vocabulary is gap ko dikhane lage hain. OpenAI finance apps se lekar talk shows tak har cheez khareed rahi hai, jabki ek shoe company ne apne aap ko AI infrastructure provider ke roop mein rebrand kiya hai.

Tokenmaxxing ka naya vocabulary bhi is gap ko dikhata hai. Tokenmaxxing ka matlab hai ki log apne skills aur knowledge ko tokenized karen, taki unhein zyada pesa mil sake. Lekin yeh sawal uthata hai ki kya hum sabhi logon ko yeh tokenmaxxing sikhane ki zaroorat hai?

OpenAI ki shopping spree bhi is gap ko dikhata hai. OpenAI har cheez khareed rahi hai, chahe wo finance apps ho ya talk shows. Lekin kya yeh sahi hai? Kya humein yeh cheezein khareedni chahiye?

Iske alawa, AI Anxiety Gap ko kam karne ke liye humein kuch karna hoga. Humein logon ko AI ke baare mein zyada se zyada batana hoga, taki ve iske baare mein achhe se samajh sakein. Humein AI ki safety aur security ke baare mein bhi logon ko batana hoga, taki ve iske baare mein chintit na hon.

Ant mein, AI Anxiety Gap ko kam karne ke liye humein sabhi logon ko saath lekar chalna hoga. Humein logon ko AI ke baare mein zyada se zyada batana hoga, aur unhein iske baare mein samjhana hoga. Tabhi hum AI Anxiety Gap ko kam kar sakte hain.
