Teen saal purana startup Factory ne $150 million raise kiya hai, jo ki Khosla Ventures dwara lead kiya gaya hai. Is funding ke baad, Factory ki valuation $1.5 billion ho gayi hai. 

 Factory ka main goal hai enterprise ke liye AI coding build karna, jisse unki productivity badh sake. Factory ke founder ka kehna hai ki unki technology se enterprises apne software development process ko automate kar sakte hain, jisse time aur cost dono ki bachat hogi. 

 Factory ki technology mein machine learning aur natural language processing ka use kiya gaya hai, jisse coding process ko automate kiya ja sakta hai. Isse developers ko apne time ko aur better cheejon par focus karne ka mauka milta hai, jaise ki software ki design aur testing. 

 Khosla Ventures ke partner ka kehna hai ki Factory ki technology mein bahut potential hai, aur unhein yakeen hai ki Factory enterprise ke liye AI coding ka future banane mein safal hogi. 

 Factory ki yeh funding enterprise ke liye AI coding ke future ko darshati hai, aur yeh bhi darshata hai ki investors ko bhi is technology mein bahut umeed hai. Hum dekhenge ki Factory aage kya karta hai, lekin abhi ke liye yeh clear hai ki Factory ne ek bahut bada kadam uthaya hai.
