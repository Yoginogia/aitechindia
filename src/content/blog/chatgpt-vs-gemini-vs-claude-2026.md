## 🧠 1. ChatGPT 5 (OpenAI)

### Strengths 💪
- **Coding** mein sabse reliable — complex bugs solve karta hai
- **DALL-E 3** se images bana sakte ho
- **Sora** se videos generate kar sakte ho
- **Plugins + GPTs** — custom AI assistants bana sakte ho
- Sabse bada user base — community support best

### Weaknesses 😐
- Free version limited hai (GPT-4o mini)
- Kabhi kabhi hallucinate karta hai (galat info deta hai)
- Response length kabhi chhoti hoti hai

### Best For:
**Coding, app development, image generation, general tasks**

---

## 🌟 2. Gemini 2.5 Pro (Google)

### Strengths 💪
- **Fastest** response time — almost instant
- **Google ecosystem** integration (Gmail, Docs, YouTube)
- **1M+ token context** — bahut lamba document ek baar mein process karega
- **Hindi support best** — Indian languages mein sabse achha
- **Free version** bahut powerful hai
- **Imagen 3** se high-quality images
- **Real-time web search** baked in

### Weaknesses 😐
- Coding mein ChatGPT/Claude se thoda peeche
- Creative writing average hai
- Kabhi over-cautious hota hai (refuse karta hai)

### Best For:
**Research, Hindi content, Google integration, fast queries, students**

---

## 🎯 3. Claude Opus 4 (Anthropic)

### Strengths 💪
- **Writing quality** sabse best — natural, human-like
- **Longest responses** — detailed explanations deta hai
- **Coding** mein ChatGPT ke barabar ya better
- **Reasoning** extremely strong — logical analysis best
- **Safety** focused — galat info dene se bachta hai
- PDF/document analysis outstanding

### Weaknesses 😐
- **No image generation** — sirf text
- **No video generation**
- Hindi support weak hai (English mein best)
- Free version mein limited messages

### Best For:
**Writing, analysis, long documents, coding, professional work**
