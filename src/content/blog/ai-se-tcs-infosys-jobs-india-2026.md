India ka IT sector ek bade crisis ka samna kar raha hai — ya phir revolution ka? 🤔

**TCS, Infosys, Wipro** — ye sab companies AI ki wajah se apni hiring policies badal rahi hain. Freshers ke liye jobs kam ho rahi hain, aur kaal ka jo "safe job" tha, woh aaj unsafe lagti hai. Lekin kya yeh picture poori hai?

---

## 📉 Kya Ho Raha Hai?

**April 2026 mein:**
- IT job openings mein **8% ki girawat** March se compare karke
- TCS ne is quarter **sirf 625 freshers** hire kiye (pehle thousands hote the)
- Infosys ne **AI-first strategy** announce ki — matlab AI pehle, manpower baad mein
- Wipro ne bhi **AI centers of excellence** shuru kiye hain

> "Ek AI system 10 junior developers ka kaam kar sakta hai" — Infosys CTO

---

## 💡 Kya Khatam Ho Rahi Hai?

Haan, kuch roles **definitely shrink** ho rahi hain:

| Role | Risk Level |
|---|---|
| Manual Testing | 🔴 HIGH |
| Data Entry / BPO | 🔴 VERY HIGH |
| Routine Code Documentation | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| Basic Web Development | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| Junior Software Engineer | 🟡 MEDIUM |

---

## ✅ Kya Safe Hai?

Lekin saath mein **naye roles bhi ban rahe hain:**

| Naya Role | Demand |
|---|---|
| AI/ML Engineer | 🚀 VERY HIGH |
| Prompt Engineer | 🚀 HIGH |
| AI Ethics Specialist | 📈 Growing |
| Cloud + AI Integration | 🚀 HIGH |
| Cybersecurity (AI-driven) | 📈 Growing |

---

## 🎓 Freshers Ke Liye Kya Karna Chahiye?

Agar aap abhi student ho ya fresher ho, toh **ab se hi prepare karlo:**

1. **Python + AI/ML** seekho — free resources: Coursera, Google AI, NPTEL
2. **ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot** use karna seekho — interview mein puchha jayega
3. **Cloud certifications** lo — AWS, Azure, Google Cloud
4. **Portfolio banao** — GitHub par AI projects daalo

---

## 🔮 2027 Tak Kya Hoga?

Industry experts ka kehna hai:
- Total IT jobs number **5-10% decrease** hoga
- Lekin AI-skilled workers ki **demand 40% increase** hogi
- Average salary for AI roles: **₹15-25 LPA** vs regular developer ₹6-12 LPA

---

## 💬 Hamaari Raay

Yeh panic karne ka time nahi hai — **upskill karne ka time hai!** AI IT sector ko khatam nahi kar raha, sirf **transform** kar raha hai. Jo log adapt karenge, woh pehle se zyada earn karenge.
