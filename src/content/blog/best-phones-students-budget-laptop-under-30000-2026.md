## 💡 Buying Tips for Students

* **SSD zaruri hai** — HDD wala laptop mat lo, slow lagega
* **8GB RAM minimum** — 4GB mein Windows 11 dhang se nahi chalta
* **Full HD display** — HD (1366x768) mat lo, text blurry dikhta hai
* **Flipkart/Amazon sale** ka wait karo — ₹3-5,000 aur kam milega!
* **Student discount** check karo — HP, Dell, Lenovo sab dete hain

## ⚠️ Kya Nahi Milega ₹30,000 Mein?

* ❌ Gaming performance (GPU nahi hogi)
* ❌ Video editing (RAM aur processor limited)
* ❌ Premium build (mostly plastic body)
* ❌ Thunderbolt/USB4 ports

**Lekin** — college work, coding, online classes, browsing, Netflix — **sab smooth chalega!** 💪
