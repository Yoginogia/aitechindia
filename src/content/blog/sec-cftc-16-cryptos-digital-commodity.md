**Breaking News!** US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) और Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) ने **17 March 2026** को एक historic 68-page joint interpretation जारी की है। 🎉

---

## 📋 क्या हुआ?

SEC और CFTC ने **16 crypto assets** को officially **"Digital Commodities"** classify किया है। इसका मतलब — ये securities नहीं हैं!

## 💡 Iska Matlab Kya Hai?

1. **Staking, Mining, Airdrops** — Securities law ke bahar! Ab exchanges aur validators ko SEC se darna nahi padega
2. **Clear Regulatory Framework** — Companies ko pata hai kya legal hai aur kya nahi
3. **Institutional Investment** — Banks aur hedge funds ab zyada confidently invest kar sakte hain
4. **Exchange Listings** — US exchanges pe in 16 cryptos ki listing aur easy hogi
