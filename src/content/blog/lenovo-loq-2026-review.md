Legion series Lenovo ki premium gaming series hai, aur LOQ (Ideapad gaming ka replacement) uski wahi Legion jaisi design language aur features bohot affordable price pe laayi hai.

## 1. The Sweet Spot Combo
AMD Ryzen 7 7840HS ke sath RTX 4050 6GB (95W TGP) ek perfectly balanced setup hai jisme CPU bottlenecks zero ke barabar hain. Is combo ki vajah se Valorant wagera asani se 300+ FPS nikal lete hain.

## 2. MUX Switch and AI Engine+ 
Pichle models me MUX switch (jo direct GPU connection allow karta hai) mehnge laptops me hi aati thi. LOQ series ne MUX switch budget segment me introduce kiye hain. Iske sath inka naya 'LA1 AI chip' game khelte waqt frame drops measure karke khud fan speed and power consumption optimize kar leta hai.

## 3. Cons?
Iska screen issue zaroor hai. Lenovo ne isme 45% NTSC color gamut dia hai. Ye screen gaming me fast aur fluid toh hai par agar aap ise professionally video grading me use karna chahte hain, toh colors fike lagsakte hain.

**Final Verdict:** First-time gamers ya engineering students jo heavy softwares (AutoCAD) aur moderate-high gaming karte hain.
