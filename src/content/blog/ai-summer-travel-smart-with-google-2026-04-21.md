Yaar, ab summer aa gaya hai aur sab log travel karne ki plan banane mein lage hue hain. Toh, agar aap bhi travel karne ki soch rahe hain toh **Google** ki salah suno. Google ne 7 tarikon se travel ko smart banane ki salah di hai, jo aapke liye bohot upyogi ho sakti hai.

 Sabse pehle, **Google Maps** ka istemal karo. Yaar, **Google Maps** ki madad se aap apne destination tak pahunchne ke liye sabse accha route dhund sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi jagah par traffic ka samna karna padta hai toh **Google Maps** ki madad se aap use bhi avoid kar sakte hain.

 Dusra, **Google Translate** ka istemal karo. Yaar, agar aap kisi aise desh mein ja rahe hain jahan ki aapko language nahi aati toh **Google Translate** ki madad se aap us language ko samajh sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi cheez ko puchoona hai toh **Google Translate** ki madad se aap usko bhi pucho sakte hain.

 Teesra, **Google Trips** ka istemal karo. Yaar, **Google Trips** ki madad se aap apne travel ki planning kar sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi cheez ki zaroorat hai toh **Google Trips** ki madad se aap use bhi book kar sakte hain.

 Chauta, **Google Photos** ka istemal karo. Yaar, **Google Photos** ki madad se aap apne travel ki photos ko save kar sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi photo ko edit karna hai toh **Google Photos** ki madad se aap use bhi edit kar sakte hain.

 Paanchva, **Google Assistant** ka istemal karo. Yaar, **Google Assistant** ki madad se aap apne travel ki planning kar sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi cheez ki zaroorat hai toh **Google Assistant** ki madad se aap use bhi book kar sakte hain.

 Chhata, **Google Wallet** ka istemal karo. Yaar, **Google Wallet** ki madad se aap apne travel ki payment kar sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi cheez ki zaroorat hai toh **Google Wallet** ki madad se aap use bhi buy kar sakte hain.

 Saatva, **Google Flights** ka istemal karo. Yaar, **Google Flights** ki madad se aap apne travel ki flight ki booking kar sakte hain. Aur, agar aapko kisi cheez ki zaroorat hai toh **Google Flights** ki madad se aap use bhi book kar sakte hain.

 Toh, yaar, ab summer travel ko aur bhi mazedaar banane ke liye **Google** ki salah suno. Aur, agar aapko kisi cheez ki zaroorat hai toh **Google** ki madad se aap use bhi kar sakte hain.
