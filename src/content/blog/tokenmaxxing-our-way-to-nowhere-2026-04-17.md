AI insiders aur baki duniya ke beech ka gap badh raha hai, aur iska asar kharch, shak aur naye shabdon mein bhi dikhai de raha hai. OpenAI finance apps se lekar talk shows tak sab kuchh kharid rahi hai, jabki ek juton ki company ne apne aap ko AI infrastructure company ke roop mein rebrand kiya hai. 

 Yeh sab kuchh tokenmaxxing ke naam se jaana jaata hai, jo ki ek aisa trend hai jismein companies apne products aur services ko AI se jodne ki koshish karti hain, chahe unhein zaroorat ho ya na ho. 

 Tokenmaxxing ke piche ka uddeshya yeh hai ki companies apne shareholders ko yeh dikhayein ki ve AI mein invest kar rahe hain, aur isse unki market value badh sakti hai. Lekin isse ek anya samasya bhi utpann ho sakti hai, jismein companies apne actual products aur services ko bhool jati hain aur sirf AI ke baare mein baat karti hain. 

 Isse ek gap banta hai jo AI insiders aur baki duniya ke beech hota hai, jismein AI insiders ko lagta hai ki ve hi sab kuchh jaante hain, aur baki duniya ko lagta hai ki ve kuchh bhi nahin jaante. 

 Yeh gap itna badh sakta hai ki logon ko lagne lage ki AI sirf ek buzzword hai, aur iske piche koi sachchai nahin hai. 

 Isliye, humein tokenmaxxing ke bare mein sochna hoga aur yeh samajhna hoga ki kya hum AI ke bare mein sirf baat kar rahe hain, ya hum sach mein kuchh kar rahe hain.
