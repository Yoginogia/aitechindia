AI ke anusar, aaj kal ek naya shabd popular ho raha hai - Tokenmaxxing. Yeh shabd AI ke saath judi technology aur finance ki duniya mein istemaal kiya ja raha hai. Lekin kya yeh shabd sachmuch kuchh neya hai, ya sirf ek marketing gimmick hai? 

 OpenAI ki shopping spree bhi kafi charcha mein hai. Company ne finance apps se lekar talk shows tak har cheez kharidne mein busy hai. Yeh keval ek company ki shopping spree nahi hai, balki yeh AI ki duniya mein ek naye yug ki shuruaat hai. 

 Lekin AI ki duniya mein yeh sab kuchh ho raha hai, jabki baaki duniya mein log abhi bhi AI ke baare mein bahut kam jaante hain. Yeh gap itni badh rahi hai ki logon ke beech ek AI Anxiety Gap paida ho raha hai. Logon ko lagta hai ki AI unke kaam ko khatam kar degi, ya unke personal data ko nuksan pahunchaegi. 

 Ek shoe company ne apne aap ko AI infrastructure company ke roop mein rebrand kar liya hai. Yeh decision company ke liye ek naye adhyay ki shuruaat hai, lekin yeh sawal bhi uthata hai ki kya yeh sirf ek marketing gimmick hai, ya sachmuch company AI mein kuchh naya karne ki koshish kar rahi hai. 

 AI ki duniya mein aaj kal bahut kuchh ho raha hai, lekin yeh sab kuchh sirf AI insiders ke liye hai. Baaki duniya ko abhi bhi AI ke baare mein bahut kuchh seekhne ki zaroorat hai. Tabhi yeh AI Anxiety Gap kam ho sakta hai, aur log AI ki sachchai ko samajh sakte hain.
