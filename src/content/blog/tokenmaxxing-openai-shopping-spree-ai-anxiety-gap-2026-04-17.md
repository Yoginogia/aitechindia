AI ka craze aaj kal har jagah hai, aur iska asar humari zindagi ke har pehlu par dikh raha hai. Lekin kya aapko pata hai ki AI insiders aur baaki duniya ke beech ka gap badh raha hai? Haan, yeh gap itna badh gaya hai ki ab humein iske liye naye shabdon ka istemaal karna pad raha hai, jaise ki 'Tokenmaxxing'.

 Tokenmaxxing ka matlab hai ki log apne AI models ko itna powerful banane ki koshish kar rahe hain ki ve har tarah ki information ko process kar sakein. Lekin iske liye unhein bahut saari cheezein kharidni pad rahi hain, jaise ki finance apps, talk shows, aur bahut kuch. OpenAI ismein sabse aage hai, aur ve har cheez kharidne mein busy hain.
