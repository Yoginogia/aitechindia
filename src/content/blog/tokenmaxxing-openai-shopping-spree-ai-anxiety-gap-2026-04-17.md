AI ke jagat mein ek naya buzzword hai - Tokenmaxxing. Yah ek aisa process hai jisme AI models ko maximize kiya jata hai taaki ve apne performance ko badha saken. Lekin yah sirf ek naya terminology nahi hai, balki yah AI insiders aur baaki duniya ke beech badhte hue gap ko bhi darshaata hai.

 OpenAI ka shopping spree bhi is gap ko darshaa raha hai. Company har tarah ki cheezon ko khareed rahi hai, jaise ki finance apps, talk shows, aur anya. Yah dikhata hai ki OpenAI apne AI models ko aur bhi powerful banane ke liye kisi bhi had tak jaane ko taiyar hai.

 Lekin yah gap sirf OpenAI tak hi seemit nahi hai. Ek shoe company ne haal hi mein apne aap ko AI infrastructure company ke roop mein rebrend kiya hai. Yah ek aisa kadam hai jo dikhata hai ki companies ab AI ko ek mahatvapoorn hissa ke roop mein dekh rahi hain.

 AI anxiety gap ko kam karne ke liye, humein AI ke baare mein adhik jaankari leni hogi. Humein samajhna hoga ki AI kya hai, kaise kaam karta hai, aur kaise hum iska upyog kar sakte hain. Sirf tab hi hum AI ke faayde ko samajh sakenge aur is gap ko kam kar payenge.
