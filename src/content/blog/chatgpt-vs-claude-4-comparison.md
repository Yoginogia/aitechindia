Yaar, Tech duniya mein toh jese toofan aa gaya hai! Pichle hi din OpenAI ne **ChatGPT-4.5** launch kiya, aur dusri taraf **Anthropic** ne **Claude 4** release kar diya 🔥

तो agar aap soch rahe ho ki "Mere use ke liye konsa behtar hai?", toh ye post bilkul aapke liye hai! Humne dono ko test kiya aur result sach mein hairan karne wale hain।

### ChatGPT-4.5 ki Khaasiyat 🤖

Bhai, OpenAI ne ek baat toh confirm kar di hai — **Speed!** Naya system purane version se **3x zyada tez** kaam karta hai. 
* **Coding:** Agar aap ek programmer ho, toh ye bina kisi error ke hazaron lines ka code second mein likh deta है।
* **Voice Mode:** Hindi aur Hinglish voice samajhne mein ye ab **99% accurate** hai। Maza aa gaya!

### Claude 4 kahan aage hai? 💡

Dusri taraf, Anthropic ne focus kiya hai "**Understanding**" par. Agar aap bahut badi PDF books ya research papers padhte hain, toh Claude 4 ek baar mein **300K tokens** (kareeb puri ek book) padh sakta hai.
Jo cheez Claude ko alag banati hai wo hai iska **natural aur human-like response**। Ye aise baat karta hai jaise koi expert dost aapko samjha raha ho।

### Kisko chunein?

Dekho seedhi si baat hai:
1. Agar aapko **Data, Coding aur fast answers** chahiye — toh seedha **ChatGPT-4.5** use karein.
2. Agar aapko **lambe Articles, Research aur summarization** chahiye — toh **Claude 4** se zyada badiya koi nahi.

Dono hi companies ab bilkul free limit ke sath naye features provide kar rahi hain। Aap inme se kaunsa AI tool apne daily work ke liye pasand karte hain?
