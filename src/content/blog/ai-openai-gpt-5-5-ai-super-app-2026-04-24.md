**Breaking News!** OpenAI ne **GPT-5.5** officially launch kar diya hai aur ye model company ko **"AI Super App"** banane ke goal ke ek step aur kariba le jaata hai। Ye sirf ek AI model nahi hai — ye OpenAI ki poori strategy ko represent karta hai ki ChatGPT ko ek **all-in-one AI platform** banana hai।

## 🚀 GPT-5.5 Mein Kya Naya Hai?

GPT-5.5 ko GPT-5 ke upar ek significant upgrade ke roop mein release kiya gaya hai। Major improvements:

### 1. Reasoning Ability — 3x Better
GPT-5.5 ka reasoning ability pichle version se **3 guna better** hai। Iska matlab:
* Complex math problems **accurately** solve karta hai
* Logical puzzles mein **fewer mistakes**
* Multi-step reasoning — lamba problem step by step solve karta hai
* Scientific reasoning mein **PhD-level performance**

### 2. Speed — 2x Faster
Response time **aadhi** ho gayi hai:
* Simple queries: **under 1 second**
* Complex analysis: **3-5 seconds** (pehle 8-10 seconds lagta tha)
* Code generation: **real-time streaming** — type karte jao, code aata jaye

### 3. Context Window — 256K Tokens
Ab aap **256,000 tokens** tak ka context de sakte ho — ye approximately:
* **200 pages** ka document ek saath analyze kar sakta hai
* Poori **book** ka summary ek prompt mein
* **Entire codebase** ko samajh sakta hai

### 4. Multi-Modal Mastery
Text, image, audio, video — sab ek saath:
* **Photo upload** karke detailed analysis lo
* **Voice conversation** naturally karo
* **Video clips** analyze karwao
* **Documents** (PDF, spreadsheets) directly process karo

### 5. Memory — Long-Term
GPT-5.5 ab **pichli conversations yaad rakhta hai**:
* Aapki preferences remember karta hai
* Work style samajhta hai over time
* Personalized responses deta hai
* Basically aapka **personal AI** ban jaata hai jo aapko jaanta hai
